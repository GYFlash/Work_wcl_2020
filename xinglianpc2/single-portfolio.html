QINGDAO STAR LINKS MACHINERY AND EQUIPMENT CO.LTD

Qingdao star links machinery and equipment CO.LTD. is an export company of various industrial and civil comprehensive machinery and equipment, as well as different types of raw materials such as doors and pipe building materials. Since its establishment ten years ago, it has established friendly relations with many countries and regions around the world Winning trade relations, the main export markets are African countries, including Algeria, Egypt, Nigeria and other countries.

The company aims to be people-oriented, operate with integrity, teamwork, tolerate innovation and benefit the society.

Mission vision: Use our high-quality products and rigorous and efficient services to gain the trust and support of more customers and become an international company with global influence.

The company has a professional supply chain management and customs export team composed of highly educated and high-quality talents. It has accumulated rich experience in exporting products including shipping and air transportation, and formed a scientific management system. The team members are young and energetic, innovative and courageous to meet challenges, rigorous and efficient, and have a good sense of social responsibility.
